Mission Statement: At Cyber Guard Research Lab, our mission is to safeguard emerging platforms, medical applications, web frameworks and other widely-used technologies by uncovering software vulnerabilities, performing measurement analysis and developing robust defense solutions. Through the utilization of cutting-edge tools powered by program analysis, machine learning, natural language processing, and LLM, we diligently identify potential threats and propose innovative strategies to fortify the cyber-physical ecosystem. Our commitment lies in ensuring the safety and security of these platforms, ultimately contributing to a more resilient digital landscape.
